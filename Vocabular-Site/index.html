Vocabular

A simple, clutter-free pocket dictionary

Change the way you lookup words and retain the meaning of them. Vocabular is your simple ad-free solution to learning new words and expanding your vocabulary.

Explore

Search for words as you would on the web. Expand your vocabulary by viewing related words.

Bookmark

Bookmark words to have them saved locally and accessible right in your homescreen.

Archive

Finish learning words and archive them. Archived words can be retrieved in the future.

Phrases

Phrases are sentences containing a particular word. Create and view phrases for words you are learning to improve your understanding of a word.
